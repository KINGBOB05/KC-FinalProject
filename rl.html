Basic mechanics

Boost Management

  • Boost makes your car go faster.

  • You can run out of boost that is why you need to know how to manage it.

  • You can collect either the pads or the megas.

Recovery

  • Recovery is one of the most important mechanics.
  • You can use your powerslide to recover well.
  • when combining the powerslide and the second flip it will result in a good Recovery.

Aerial Control

  • When aerial control is excuted perfectly it will make your car in the best spot to hit a ball.

  • The training that is shown in the video will help you with that.
